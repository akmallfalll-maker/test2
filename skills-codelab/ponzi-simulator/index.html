The Pyramid

How it Works

Phase 1: The Setup. You are the founder. Recruit new investors by promising them high returns (e.g., 20% a month). You will use the money from new investors to pay off the early investors, keeping a cut for yourself.

The Collapse

The scheme has collapsed! You no longer have enough funds to pay the required returns to your investors.

This is the mathematical reality of a Ponzi scheme: it requires an exponentially growing base of new investors to sustain the payouts for earlier ones. Once recruitment slows, the system breaks.
